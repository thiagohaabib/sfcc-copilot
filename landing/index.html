Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

💬

Natural language input

Describe promotions the way you'd explain them to a colleague. No SFCC jargon required.

⚡

Instant creation

Campaign, promotion, customer group, and coupon — all created and linked in one action via OCAPI.

🔍

Lookup existing records

Ask for details of any promotion or campaign by ID. Get structured info without opening the BM form.

🎟️

Smart coupon handling

Copilot detects when a campaign already has Everyone group and warns you before making the coupon redundant.

🔒

Your credentials, your data

SFCC credentials are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is shared or stored on our servers.

🌐

Works on any SFCC instance

Compatible with any SFCC sandbox or production environment running OCAPI Data API v24+.
